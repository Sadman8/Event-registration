About The Event

The Industry-Academia Collaboration Summit-2025 themed "Bridging the Gap: Enhancing Employability through Industry-Academia Collaboration" is a groundbreaking initiative by Daffodil International University (DIU) aimed at fostering stronger ties between academic institutions and industry leaders. This event serves as a platform for dialogue, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration to address the critical gaps between education and employability. Designed as a two-day summit, the event will feature keynote speeches, interactive panel discussions, workshops, exhibitions, and networking opportunities, making it a comprehensive engagement for all stakeholders.
